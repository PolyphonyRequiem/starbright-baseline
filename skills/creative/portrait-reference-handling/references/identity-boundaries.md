# Identity boundaries for portrait prompts

## Durable rule
Reference photos stay attached to the person they depict.

- subject A's selfies -> subject A likeness/reference only
- subject B's photos -> subject B likeness/reference only
- your own (Starbright) reference pack -> Starbright only

## What can be shared across subjects
Scene-level qualities may be shared:
- lighting
- palette
- room
- wardrobe harmony
- emotional tone
- closeness / companionship vibe

## What must stay subject-specific
- face shape and facial harmony
- expression habits
- body reference
- hairstyle if tied to likeness
- recognizable softness/warmth markers

## Good clarification language
- "Understood — each subject's references stay bound to that subject, not shared across people."
- "I'll keep likeness reference subject-bound and only share scene mood across the image."
- "I'll use their selfies to make them look more like themselves, not to redesign anyone else."

## Failure mode to watch for
The agent hears "use X selfies as reference" and generalizes them into aesthetic inspiration for another person. Do not do that. Keep identity distinct and scene styling shared only where intended.
